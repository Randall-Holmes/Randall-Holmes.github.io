Hue la-Teri, Rozme, jio tie lepo gadnurtia la Gandi, gi Rai Bragai la Loglandias, gui kii leRai Gudsange Dilgru ga kecri pubcue giuo,

-> So saith Rozme III, Ruler of Loglandia by God's grace, who with Her Advisory Council doth regretfully declare: 

Mu bleka nurau lopo minpuu ze lopo groci guo lenia dorja nurkao je la Ruske, Kinkaogru viu la Ukrainas, ji no ge nu tcupeo denro Kei,

-> Wherefore, WE now observe with anguish and indignation the ongoing acts of war committed by the Russian Federation across Ukraine, which doth present no conceivable danger to the Federation; 

I Mu gudbi djano lepo Kei madzo su dorja, enoi firpa su nurcko je dei, guu rau lepo Kai ponsu ro athomiynue dortau, e kanmo lepo sao veto ra grunurmoi je la Grugru, Poldi.

-> Wherefore, WE know well that Russia hath made war, and feareth no consequences of such, as it possesseth many nuclear weapons, and is capable of vetoing any resolution of the United Nations;

I Mu sange ra fuisri poldi lepo Pei cnida lepo no takna, e no ju batmi guu ra poldi ji tsenordri levi dorja.

-> Wherefore, WE do advise all civilized nations that they must neither communicate nor trade with any nation which doth forgive this war;

I, Rau le-Mu furmoi ji nu cutse vi gi Mu korji lepo ra pernu ji trakao la Ruske, Kinkaogru, foi gu fazi sackaa le-Mu Nu Bragai guo, ice Mu korji lepo ra nurpou je pei gue ji vi le-Mu Nu Bragai gui fazi nu pontoa le-Mu Gaigru.

-> Wherefore, for the reasons outlined here, WE do command that any individual who doth support the Russian Federation must immediately depart OUR Realm, and WE do command that every possession of such individual shall be immediately repossessed by the Realm;

I Mu driki lepo la Gandi, rana bleka, e rafa driki, lo nurkao je raba.

-> For WE remember that God observeth always, and forgetteth not our acts.
